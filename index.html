For the duration of the HfbK annual exhibition 2023 from the 9th to the 12th of february The Variety Room opens its doors in room 123 to showcase performance based work of HfbK Students from all classes and departments.


Our curatorial duties will solely consist of creating the time table for the program and decorating the room.



The varietyroom offers a moody space, a basic stage of 2 by 3 meters, 2 spotlights, a beamer, 2 microphones, 2 chairs, a sound system and an 8 channel mixer with 6.25 audiojack connections. Please consult floorplan below for room layout.





Please include in your concept:

  • names and classes of all participants (for hfbk website)
  • title, a short description and an approximate duration of your performance
  • optional: additonal media or links that give us more details about your performance
  • optional: if you need additional equipment please include that. Important: this is only for our planning, please bring all additional equipment you require with you


We are looking forward to your performance. Please send all required information to hi@varietyroom.de by 28.01.2023.

If you have any questions write us an email as well or hit up Apollonia, Lion or Johannes from the Angela Bulloch class.
